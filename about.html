Company Profile

Kingdom Lubricants Factory L.L.C, an ISO 9001-2008 certified company, is a Grease & Oil manufacturing company. Since its conception, has been on an escalator of success with many wise minds and expert hands behind it. Synonymous with perfection, Kingdom Lubricants is committed to manufacture best automotive lubricants in United Arab Emirates (UAE) along with a strong foothold in grease and speciality products. With its product development approach, state of the art manufacturing capability and a highly committed workforce, the organization provides best-in-class petroleum products. Kingdom Lubricants performance and consistency has earned it the trust from all customers across the globe. Appreciating the trust and preference of its customers, the company aspires to further become the largest private lubricants & Greases manufacturer in United Arab Emirates (UAE). Leading the future of lubricants industries, Kingdom Lubricants Factory L.L.C. with the use of 100% virgin Base Oil and superior quality imported additives, offers the ‘best-in-class’ products to its customers. The products meet or exceed the quality requirements of international organizations such as: SAE, ACEA, API, ISO and NLGI, OEM (Volvo) approvals.
