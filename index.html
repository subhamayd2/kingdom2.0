Kingdom Lubricants

Kingdom Lubricants Lubricants Factory LLC , an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Industrial Oils, Lubricants and Greases manufacturer. Since our establishment in 1990, we have been commited to provide customised lubrication solutions to many industries and clients.We, at Kingdom Lubricants, GUARANTEE smooth and prompt delivery of our products in every part of the globe because of our concentration in one activity of Lubricants manufacturing.We manufacture speciality lubricants across several domains such as aviation, automobile, paper mills, printing press, power plants, cement and construction, tyre manufacturing and general manufacturing industries. synthetic oils, semi-synthetic oils and greases, high performance industrial lubricants and graphite based lubricants are our key area of excellence.The goals and objectives of Kingdom Lubricants is to engage efficiently, responsibly and profitably in the oil business.
